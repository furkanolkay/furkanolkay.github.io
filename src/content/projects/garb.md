Garb is an innovative wardrobe management application that helps you organize your clothing items and create stylish outfits effortlessly. With Garb, you can catalog your entire wardrobe, mix and match pieces to create new looks, and plan your outfits ahead of time. The app's intelligent styling suggestions help you make the most of your existing clothes while reducing decision fatigue and saving you time each morning.
